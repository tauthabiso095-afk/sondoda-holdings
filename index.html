About Sondoda Holdings

Sondoda Holdings is a South African black-owned diversified company focused on construction, logistics, engineering, plant hire and community-driven development solutions.

Founded on the principles of excellence, empowerment and innovation, the company is committed to creating sustainable economic opportunities while delivering world-class services across public and private sectors.

Our vision is to become a leading African infrastructure and industrial solutions group that transforms communities through strategic partnerships, employment creation and impactful projects.
